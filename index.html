  • traditional developers are cautious due to uncertainty of commercial / residential mismatch

  • VR
  • AI
  • Blockchain
  • Making, Tools, Engineering
  • Web Development

As well as soft skills that are most valuable in life:

  • Entrepreneurship
  • Communication
  • Cultures + Languages
  • Relationships
  • Health + Wellness + Nutrition
  • Concentration + Focus

Holistic curriculum that will ensure prosperity in the post-work era when majority of the jobs are automated.

  • Global warming
  • Air polution
  • Plastic recycling
  • Deforestation

We appreciate that ICO and cryptocurrencies are volatile - we invest in real estate and education - chances are that some of it will preserve its value.
