Architecting Earth 2.0
Many things will have to change, these are the most impactful and critical points, attainable right now:
  1. Land use. Food system. Less crops to feed livestock. Rebuilding soil and natural ecosystems.
  2. Emissions Trading System. No more free allowances. Unified global standard supported by enforceable sanctions.
  3. Energy storage and transmission. You can install solar panels but it will not reduce the dependency on fossil fuels - we are using peaker plants to supply electricity during peak demand.
The book covers many other things and many interdependencies in more detail.
